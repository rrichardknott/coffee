Tamper Care Instructions

Your Wiedemann Precision Tamper is made of high quality materials. The base is made of stainless steel, the handle of wood or aluminum. Wood is a natural material and changes its appearance with time and use. Protect your Precision Tamper from water and simply clean it with a damp kitchen towel. Never put it in the dishwasher. The same applies to the Tamper with aluminium handle.

What size do I need? Options "58.0mm, 58.5mm" fit for: If you own a machine model from the following manufacturers and use their supplied 58mm filter baskets , our Precision Tamper 58 is an ideal fit: Bezzera, BFC ,Brasilia ,Carimali, Conti, ECM, Elektra, Faema, Fiorenzato, Gaggia, Grimac, IIsomac, IZZO, La Cimbali, La Marzocco, Nuova Simonelli, Quickmill, Rancilio, Rocket Espresso Milano, Vibiemme, Wega

What is the difference between option "58.0mm" and option "58.5mm"? These specifications are the lower diameter of the tamper plate. Both fit in 58s filter baskets. Which of the two options is used is, like so many things in life, a matter of taste. If the Tamper is used by different people and if it is sometimes better to go faster, we recommend the 58.0mm Tamper. If the preparation is celebrated and the ideal result is particularly important, we recommend the 58.5mm Tamper. When using this diameter, the ground coffee in your sieve is compacted a little further towards the edge and the risk of small channels (so-called channelling) forming in the coffee puck due to the brewing pressure is minimised. Option "41mm" fits for: The Precision Funnel 41 was specially developed for the Precision Tamper 41 and can only be used with it. Together they perfect your espresso preparation with the LM 7g Strada filter. The La Marzocco LM 7g Strada filter is required for use.

How do I know if I have a 58mm strainer? The 58mm is the inner diameter of the strainer. Just take your portafilter out of the brew group and measure it as shown in the picture. Important: If the inner diameter is just under 59mm, then it is called a 58mm strainer. The sizes "58.0mm and 58.5mm" fit for this filter baskets.
